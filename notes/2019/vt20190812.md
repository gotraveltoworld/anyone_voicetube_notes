## learning points
1. _
	* equal [ˋikwəl] (v.) 等於；相等
		- 1 + 1 equals one
		- All men are created equal.
		- equality => n. 注意發音

2. _
	* crowd [kraʊd] (n.) 人群；群衆
		- The crowd gave the actors a standing ovation at the end of the musical.
			+ 群衆在演唱會結束時給演員起立鼓掌。
		- Thousands of people crowded the arena where the major pop starter was throwing a concert.
			+ 上千人擠滿了流行音樂正在舉辦音樂會的舞台。
		- Heavy traffic and crowded streets are two characteristics of a big city.
			+ 繁忙的交通和擁擠的街道是大城市的兩大特色。

3. _
	* muffle [ˋmʌf!] (v.) 使（聲音）變微弱（或低沉）；消（聲）
		- The earmuffs are worn to muffle loud noises and to protect ears.
			+ 佩戴耳罩以消除大聲噪音並保護耳朵。

4. _
	* revenue [ˋrɛvə͵nju] (n.) 收益；收入
		- A lot of websites generate Revenue through placing advertisements on their site.
			+ 許多網站通過在其網站上放置廣告來產生收入。

5. _
	* opportunity [͵ɑpɚˋtjunətɪ] (n.) 機會；機遇
		- Participating in a school club is a great opportunity to make new friends that have the same interest
			+ 參加學校俱樂部是結交具有相同興趣的新朋友的絕佳機會。

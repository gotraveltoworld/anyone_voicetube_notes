## learning points
1. _
	* payment  [ˈpeɪ.mənt] (n.) 支付的金額
		- I haven't received your payment yet can you have a look into that?
			+ 我尚未收到您的付款，您可以看看嗎？

2. _
	* eventually [ɪˋvɛntʃʊəlɪ] (adv.) 最終，終於
		- I've been living in Taiwan for more than 10 years now but eventually I want to try things out abroad.
			+ 我已經在台灣生活了10多年，但最終我想在國外嘗試一些東西。

3. _
	* own [on] (v.) （合法）擁有
		- I don't own any rights to this footage.
			+ 我沒有此影片的任何權利。

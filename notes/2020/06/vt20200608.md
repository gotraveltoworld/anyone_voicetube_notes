## learning points
1. _
	* derogatory [dɪˋrɑgə͵torɪ] (adj.) 貶低的；詆毀的
		- he made some derogatory comments about her appearance.
2. _
	* slur [slɝ] (n.) 侮辱；毀謗
		- Racial slurs aren't funny at all
3. _
	* race [res] (n.) 人種；種族
		- people of many different races are living side-by-side in peace.
4. _
	* issue [ˋɪʃjʊ] (n.) 問題；爭議

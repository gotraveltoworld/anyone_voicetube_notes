## learning points
1. _
	* tuck [tʌk] (v.) 把…夾入；把…藏入；把…塞入
	* tuck in [tʌk] (v.) 盡情地吃，痛快地吃
		- we made plenty of food today so please enjoy yourself and tucked in.
2. _
	* Walk someone out [] (phr. .)

		- I'm going to walk my dinner off

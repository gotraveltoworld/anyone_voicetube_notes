	* footstep  [ˋfʊt͵stɛp] (n.) 腳步，步伐；腳步聲
		- I heard footsteps behind me but when I turned around there was no one there.

2. _
	* flicker  [ˋflɪkɚ] (v.) 閃爍
		- the lights in the house started to flicker during the earthquake.

3. _
	* physical  [ˋfɪzɪk!] (adj.) 身體的

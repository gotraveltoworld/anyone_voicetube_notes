# Topic

>【艾倫秀】超爆笑！人生很難當媽更難 (Mom of Two Ali Wong Has Suffered Enough)<br>
> That's my favorite mantra when it comes to motherhood. I have suffered enough.<br>
> 當談到為人母這件事時，這是我最喜歡說的口頭禪：我受夠了。<br>
